Irradiance Environment Map

Actually, you might have seen this in the previous images that the diffuse material actually could be lightened by the irradiance environment map. We have implemented a functionality that can let us see the irradiance environment map more easily. Simply press i and you can toggle between the normal environment map and the irradiance one.

Let's see some picture of diffuse objects that the scene has completely no light:

It is also remarkable that in the last image, the SSAO is helping the scene to create lots of reasonable shadows while there's actually no light. When adding light to the scene, the irradiance environment map is behaving even better to give the scene a variety of colors.
